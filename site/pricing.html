Questions

Frequently asked

Do I have to use your payment processor?

No. Tavo is processor-agnostic — connect Valor, Stripe, and others, and keep your own merchant account and negotiated rates. We don't add a markup.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract and no early-termination fee. Pay month to month and cancel anytime.

How long does it take to switch?

Most single locations are live in an afternoon. We help import your menu or product catalog so you're not retyping everything.

What happens if the internet goes down?

Tavo keeps ringing up orders and cash sales offline, then syncs automatically when you're back online.

Does it work for retail, not just restaurants?

Yes. Flip to retail mode for a barcode-first register with SKUs, weighted items, and stock control — same system, same login.

Can I run multiple locations?

Yes. The Enterprise tier rolls up sales, inventory, and staff across all your locations.
